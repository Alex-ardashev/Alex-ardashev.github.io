Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account directly within the app settings under "Edit Profile" > "Delete Account".

Can I get a refund for a failed commitment?
Penalty charges are generally non-refundable as they are processed immediately. However, if there was a technical error verifyng your activity, please contact us within 48 hours.
