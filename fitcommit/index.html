Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account directly within the app settings under "Edit Profile" > "Delete Account".

How do I cancel my FitCommit Pro subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to your device Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions to manage or cancel.

What happens if I miss a commitment?
You will have points deducted from your total score, which may affect your leaderboard ranking. No monetary charges are applied for missed commitments.
